# Useful-Hyperlinks
A curated collection of high-quality hyperlinks across data, technology, science, and regulatory affairs

This repository serves as a central index of trusted online resources covering multiple knowledge domains, including data science, data analysis, technology, scientific research, and healthcare & pharmaceutical regulatory affairs.

Rather than hosting content, this project focuses on discoverability and relevance by organizing valuable articles, journals, documentation, tools, and updates from across the web into one easily navigable space.

# Domains covered:

# Data Science & Machine Learning
Tutorials, research papers, tools, and industry blogs

# Data Analysis & Visualization
Best practices, case studies, and analytical methodologies

# Technology & Software Engineering
Platforms, frameworks, architecture insights, and trends

# Scientific Journals & Research Articles
Peer-reviewed publications, open-access journals, and academic resources

# Healthcare & Pharmaceutical Regulatory Affairs
Global regulatory updates, guidelines, policy changes, and compliance insights

# What makes this repository useful

Curated from credible and authoritative sources

Focused on recent updates, emerging trends, and practical relevance

Organized for quick reference rather than long-form reading

Useful for both learning and staying informed

# Intended audience:

Data scientists, analysts, and researchers

Technology professionals and engineers

Students and academics

Regulatory affairs professionals in healthcare and pharma

Anyone looking to stay current with reliable, cross-domain information

# Purpose:

To act as a living knowledge index, helping users cut through information overload and quickly reach high-signal, up-to-date resources across multiple disciplines.

All links point to publicly available resources. Credit and ownership remain with the original authors and publishers.
